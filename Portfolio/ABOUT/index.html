About Me

Hello, my name is Tomo. I was born and raised in Japan. After graduating from university, I worked in digital marketing and as a counsellor at a tourist information center in Japan.

I am a very passionate and curious person who pursues everything to the fullest. There are still many things that I have yet to learn, but I am eager to learn and improve my skills.
